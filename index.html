About Me

A rising senior in high school, I have had a passion for computer science and robotics since the 7th grade. For the past three years, I have been competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) and have been the Project Manager for the 3-time World Chapmion team 6299 QuadX. Along with robotics, I have mastered the art of balancing being in the top 10 of my class, maintaining various side projects, yoga, and somehow even having a social life.
