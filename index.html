Hi, I'm Linnea

I'm a college student passionate about STEM and eating pie.

If that’s a vague description, it’s because I have only a vague understanding of what exactly it is I want to do with my life. The problem is, I am an overzealous horder of academic interests. I love the problem solving of engineering, the exploration of scientific discovery, and the logical purity of mathematics. Of course, engineers also use logic, and scientists certainly solve problems. And it's the blending of professions, the links between topics, that I love. In a perfect world, my profession would involve the best of each of these fields. With that combo, I don’t think there’s anything that can't be acheived, whether it’s standardizing quantum computing, enhancing AI with big data, or creating new tools to explore the universe.
